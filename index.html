Welcome to the completely randomized card game about life.

The Most Easily Forgotten Thing is a card set inspired by Brian Eno's "Oblique Strategies," that creates quick, randomized mental health breaks. It is a low pressure, do-good tool that can be used at your own pace.

PRINT INSTRUCTIONS:

Print to standard copy paper, I suggest using cardstock, if able, for a more durable card.

Print one sided, at "Actual Size" or "Scaling: 100%,"

Then use the dotted lines to cut out your cards... NOTE: While the dotted lines do not extend to the edge of the paper, you should still be cutting to the edge.

The play guide at on the last page is a traditional one-page zine! Cut it out and follow these fold instructions:



The cards AND play guide will fit in a standard tin for storage, think Altoids. Or just throw them in a sandwhich bag! Whatever works.
